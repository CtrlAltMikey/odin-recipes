Classic Shepard's Pie Recipe

Recipe Information

Shepard's Pie is a hearty, comforting dish that combines ground meat, vegetables, and mashed potatoes. It's perfect for a family dinner or potluck.

  • Prep Time: 20 minutes
  • Cook Time: 40 minutes
  • Total Time: 1 hour
  • Servings: 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef or lamb
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 1 cup beef or vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 cups mashed potatoes
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Step 1: Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Heat a large skillet over medium heat and cook ground meat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  2. Step 2: Add onion, garlic, carrots, and peas to the skillet. Cook until vegetables are tender, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Step 3: Stir in broth, tomato paste, Worcestershire sauce, salt, and pepper. Simmer until mixture thickens, about 5 minutes.
  4. Step 4: Transfer meat mixture to a baking dish. Spread mashed potatoes evenly over the top.
  5. Step 5: Bake in preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until potatoes are lightly browned.
  6. Step 6: Optional: Sprinkle shredded cheese on top during the last 5 minutes of baking.
  7. Step 7: Let cool slightly before serving.
